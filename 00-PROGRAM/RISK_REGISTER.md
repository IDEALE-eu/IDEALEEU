# Risk Register

## Overview

This document tracks identified risks, their assessment, and mitigation strategies.

## Risk Categories

- Technical risks
- Schedule risks
- Cost risks
- Supply chain risks
- Certification risks
- Safety risks

## Risk Assessment Matrix

Risks are assessed on:
- **Probability:** Low / Medium / High
- **Impact:** Low / Medium / High
- **Risk Level:** Probability Ã— Impact

## Mitigation Strategies

Each identified risk includes:
- Risk description
- Root cause analysis
- Mitigation plan
- Owner
- Status
- Residual risk assessment
