## Review Success Criteria

The Conceptual Design Review will be considered successful when:

‚úì **Feasibility Demonstrated**: Concept viability shown through analysis  
‚úì **Requirements Validated**: Top-level requirements agreed and baselined  
‚úì **Major Risks Identified**: Key technical and programmatic risks understood  
‚úì **Architecture Selected**: System architecture chosen and justified  
‚úì **Cost and Schedule Credible**: Realistic estimates with appropriate contingency  
‚úì **Stakeholder Alignment**: Regulatory, customer, and partner buy-in achieved
