## Purpose

Monitor potential issues:
- Emerging concerns
- Trend indicators
- Warning signs
- Precursor events

## Watchlist Criteria

Items that show:
- Performance degradation
- Schedule pressure
- Quality issues
- Resource constraints

## Monitoring

- Regular reviews

- Action thresholds
