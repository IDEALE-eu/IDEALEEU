# Supplier Contracts

[← Back to SUPPLIERS](../README.md) | [↑ Up to CMP](../../README.md)

Contract agreements with composite materials suppliers.

## Purpose

Establish legal:
- Terms and conditions
- Pricing
- Delivery terms
- Warranties

## Contract Types

### Purchase Orders
- Standard items
- Fixed price
- Short lead time

### Subcontracts
- Complex work packages
- Cost-plus or fixed price
- Long-term relationships

### Framework Agreements
- Multiple releases
- Volume pricing
- Long-term capacity

## Contract Documents

- Contract agreement
- [SOW](../SOW/)
- Specifications
- Quality requirements
- Delivery schedule
